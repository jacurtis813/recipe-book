Bacon, Egg Burrito

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 4 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Bacon Strips Diced 6 Slices
Hash Potatoes Cubed 1 Cup
Eggs (Brown or White) 6 Eggs
Sour Cream 1/4 Cup
Shredded Cheddar Cheese 3/4 Cup
Hot Pepper Sauce 1 Teaspoon
Flour Tortillas (10in) 4 Tortillas

Step 1: In a large skillet, cook bacon over medium heat until crisp. Using a slotted spoon, remove to paper towels; drain, reserving 1 tablespoon drippings.

Step 2:Saute potatoes and onions in drippings until potatoes are golden brown, stirring occasionally.

Step 3: In a large bowl, whisk eggs and sour cream. Stir in 1/4 cup cheese, taco sauce and hot pepper sauce.

Step 4: Pour over potato mixture; add bacon. Cook and stir over medium heat until eggs are completely set.

Step 5: Spoon about 3/4 cup down the center of each tortilla; sprinkle with remaining cheese. Fold bottom and sides of tortilla over filling.

Step 6: Serve immediately with sour cream and tomatoes if desired.
